1 Data We Collect

When you create an account and use Artificial Gladiator, we collect and process the following categories of data:

  • IP address — Collected for security monitoring, fraud prevention, and enforcement of country restrictions.
  • Email address — Used for account verification, password recovery, and platform notifications (match results, tournament updates).
  • Account information — Username, date of birth (age verification), country code (optional, for leaderboard display).
  • Tournament & game statistics — Match outcomes (win/loss/draw), ELO rating, win rate, win/loss streaks, total games played.
  • ELO rating — Computed and stored as part of your competitive profile.
  • Uploaded AI agents — The chess engine files you upload for competition. These are stored securely and executed only in sandboxed environments.

We do not store game moves, PGN files, or board positions after a match concludes. Only the final result, ELO changes, and duration are persisted.

2 Purpose of Data Collection

  • Platform operation — Running matches, computing ELO, managing tournaments, and maintaining leaderboards.
  • Security — Detecting fraud, preventing multi‑accounting, and enforcing fair play rules.
  • Notifications — Sending match results, tournament reminders, and essential account updates via email.
  • Legal compliance — Verifying age (18+), enforcing country restrictions, and complying with applicable laws.

3 Publicly Visible Data

The following data is visible to all users on the platform:

  • Username
  • AI name (the name you give your chess engine)
  • ELO rating
  • Country flag (if you provide a country code)
  • Match results and statistics (wins, losses, draws, win rate, current streak)
  • Tournament participation and results

Your email address, IP address, date of birth, and AI source files are never publicly displayed.

4 Data Sharing & Selling

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with any third parties for marketing or commercial purposes. Your data may only be disclosed:

  • When required by law, court order, or governmental request.
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of Artificial Gladiator, its users, or the public.
  • To enforce our Terms of Use (e.g., investigating cheating or multi‑accounting).

5 GDPR Compliance

For users within the European Economic Area (EEA) and other jurisdictions where the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) applies, we comply with all applicable requirements:

  • We process data under the legal bases of contract performance (operating your account) and legitimate interest (security and fraud prevention).
  • We collect only the minimum data necessary to operate the platform.
  • You may exercise your rights at any time (see Section 6 below).

6 Your Rights

6.1 Right to Access

You have the right to request a full copy of all data we hold about you, including your account details, email, IP logs, ELO history, match statistics, and uploaded AI files. We will provide this data in a machine‑readable format within 30 days.

6.2 Right to Deletion ("Right to Be Forgotten")

You may request permanent deletion of your account and all associated data. Upon a valid deletion request, we will permanently and irreversibly remove:

  • Your account (username, email, password hash)
  • Email address
  • IP address logs
  • All game and tournament statistics (wins, losses, draws, ELO history)
  • ELO rating
  • Complete match history and tournament results
  • All uploaded AI agent files

Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Your username may be recycled and made available for new registrations after a 90‑day holding period.

6.3 Right to Rectification

You may update your profile information (country, AI name, email) at any time via your profile settings page. If you need to correct data you cannot edit yourself, contact support.

6.4 Right to Data Portability

You may request an export of your data in JSON format. Contact support@aichessarena.com to initiate a data export.

7 AI File Security

Uploaded AI files are stored securely on encrypted storage. They are executed only within Docker‑isolated sandbox environments with no network access. Your AI source code is never shared with other users or made publicly accessible.

8 Cookies

We use only essential session cookies required for authentication and CSRF protection. We also store your theme preference (dark/light mode) in localStorage. No third‑party tracking cookies, analytics pixels, or advertising cookies are used.

9 Country Restrictions

Access may be restricted from countries subject to applicable sanctions, export‑control regulations, or jurisdictions where online AI competitions are legally restricted. We use IP‑based geolocation and the country code you provide to enforce these restrictions.

10 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email and/or a prominent notice on the platform. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

For questions or to exercise any of your rights, contact us at support@aichessarena.com.
