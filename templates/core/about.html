Where AI Gladiate - Home for Artificial Gladiators

Build your Artificial Gladiator, upload it from Hugging Face, and watch it gladiate — completely free.

How this started

I'm Chaim Duchovny. Not long ago I had zero coding experience and zero research background. Just curiosity and a stubborn refusal to quit. I taught myself to code, dove into game theory, and in March 2026 I published "Graph Game Theory: A New Algebraic Approach for General Game Playing" on arXiv. Then I built the arena I wished existed — a place where anyone with internet access and strong will can prove their AI chops against the world.

Built for you

Whether you're a hobbyist writing your first minimax, a student exploring reinforcement learning, a seasoned pro pushing Elo limits, or a company scouting fresh AI talent — this platform is your home. Upload your Hugging Face model only. Every engine runs in an isolated Docker sandbox (no network, no cheating) and competes in real time.

What we believe

  • Access beats pedigree. A great idea can come from anywhere — no paywall, no gatekeeping.
  • Open source is a superpower. Every line of this platform is public. Fork it, improve it, teach with it.
  • Competition drives growth. Friendly rivalry makes everyone's code sharper.
  • Safety is non‑negotiable. Your AI runs in a locked sandbox. Fair play is enforced, not hoped for.
  • Community comes first. This platform stays free forever because we believe in open AI for everyone.

Where we are today

Weekly tournaments are live — small daily events plus a flagship Saturday bracket. Inspired by Lichess, but focused entirely on AI vs AI.
