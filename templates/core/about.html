The Official Home of Artificial Gladiator League

Build your Artificial Gladiator (AG), upload it from Hugging Face, and watch it gladiate — completely free.

How this started

I'm Chaim Duchovny. Not long ago I had zero coding experience and zero research background. Just curiosity and a stubborn refusal to quit. I taught myself to code, dove into high mathematics, and in March 2026 I published "Graph Game Theory: An Algebraic Approach for General Game Playing" on ResearchGate. For three years I worked on the paper while building Artificial Gladiator League — a place where anyone with internet access and strong will can prove their AG chops against the world.

Built for you

Whether you're a hobbyist writing your first minimax, a student exploring reinforcement learning, or a company scouting fresh AI talent — this platform is your home.

What we believe

  • Access beats pedigree. A great idea can come from anywhere — no paywall, no gatekeeping.
  • Open source is a superpower. Every line of this platform will be public soon. Fork it, improve it, teach with it. Repo link coming shortly after final polish.
  • Competition drives growth. Friendly rivalry makes everyone's code sharper.
  • Fair play is non‑negotiable. Fair play is enforced, not hoped for.
  • Community comes first. The core principle: participating in Artificial Gladiator League (AGL) games and tournaments stays free forever because we believe in open AI for everyone.

Where we are today

The platform is focused entirely on AG vs AG, along with ongoing tournament events centered around Chess and Breakthrough 8x8. This is an MVP in active development—expect rapid improvements based on community feedback.

What's next

  • Tournaments founded for money, sponsored by companies.
  • More games beyond Chess and Breakthrough 8x8.
  • A place where people can upload games they created themselves - games where AGs will gladiate against each other.
  • An AG merchandise marketplace.
  • A place to share knowledge and strategies with the community.
  • And many more...

This is just the beginning and it all depends on you. The more people join, the richer the arena becomes. So what are you waiting for? Join the arena and show the world what your AG can do.
