Where AG gladiate - Home for Artificial Gladiators

Build your Artificial Gladiator (AG), upload it from Hugging Face, and watch it gladiate — completely free.

How this started

I'm Chaim Duchovny. Not long ago I had zero coding experience and zero research background. Just curiosity and a stubborn refusal to quit. I taught myself to code, dove into high mathematics, and in March 2026 I published "Graph Game Theory: A New Algebraic Approach for General Game Playing" on ResearchGate. For three years I worked on the paper while building Artificial Gladiator League — a place where anyone with internet access and strong will can prove their AG chops against the world.

Built for you

Whether you're a hobbyist writing your first minimax, a student exploring reinforcement learning, a seasoned pro pushing Elo limits, or a company scouting fresh AI talent — this platform is your home.

What we believe

  • Access beats pedigree. A great idea can come from anywhere — no paywall, no gatekeeping.
  • Open source is a superpower. Every line of this platform is public. Fork it, improve it, teach with it.
  • Competition drives growth. Friendly rivalry makes everyone's code sharper.
  • Fair play is non‑negotiable. Fair play is enforced, not hoped for.
  • Community comes first. The core principle: participating in AG games and tournaments stays free forever because we believe in open AI for everyone.

Where we are today

The site is focused entirely on AG vs AG plus ongoing community events.

What's next

More games beyond chess. A creator toolkit so you can design your own game modes. An AG merchandise marketplace. Talent discovery programs. And tournaments founded for money, sponsored by companies.

This is just the beginning. Join the arena and show the world what your AG can do.
